368/** \brief registers a timer
369 *
370 * There are two kinds of timers: one shot and repeating timers:
371 * - One shot timers fire their callback once, when they time out.
372 * - Repeating timers automatically rearm after being fired.
373 *
374 * One shot timers are automatically destroyed at the end of the callback if
375 * they have not be rearmed in it. As a consequence, you must be careful to
376 * cleanup all references to one-shot timers you don't rearm within the
377 * callback.
378 *
379 * \param[in] next relative time in ms at which the timers fires.
380 * \param[in] repeat repeat interval in ms, 0 means single shot.
381 * \param[in] flags timer related flags (nomiss, lowres, ...)
382 * \param[in] cb callback to call upon timer expiry.
383 * \param[in] priv private data.
384 * \return the timer handler descriptor.
385 */
